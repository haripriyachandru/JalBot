Description

The Chatbot answers queries related to - water level scenario, hydrogeological scenario, water quality, available reports for an area. Additionally it generates a comprehensive report of the Aol on - Ground Water Resource Assessment, Categorization of the area, GW management practices to be adopted, Conditions for obtaining NOC for ground water extraction, guidance on how to obtain NOC, definition of groundwater terms, training opportunities related to ground water etc.

Highlights

✓ Provides precise, personalized groundwater reports using AI analytics.
✓ Multilingual Support ensures accessibility for users from diverse regions, making it easy to use for everyone.
✓ Providing voice based search to capture and process user queries via voice for seamless interaction.

Objective

✓ Aligned to Sustainable Development Goals(SDG):
Goal 6:Clean water and sanitation
SDG 6 is to: "Ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all.The Joint Monitoring Programme (JMP) of WHO and UNICEF is responsible for monitoring progress to achieve the first two targets of this goal. Important indicators for this goal are the percentages of the population that uses safely managed drinking water, and has access to safely managed sanitation. The JMP reported in 2017 that 4.5 billion people do not have safely managed sanitation.Another indicator looks at the proportion of domestic and industrial wastewater that is safely treated.
